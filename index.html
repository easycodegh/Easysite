INFORMATION ON ONLINE PROGRAMME

EasyCode's online programme seeks to engage both students and workers who have the desire to explore the world of coding to bring their creative ideas into reality. Tuition is conducted online via zoom. Prospective participants, therefore, would need a computer, download and install zoom and a reliable internet access. The entire content of the programme requires a total of fifty hours of tutor-student engagement. This translates to a period of one and a half months from start to finish.

Knowledge in basic usage of the computer will be an added advantage. There shall be few downloads and accessing some relevant websites. However, guidance will be given at all times. In web development, content is everything. Prospective participants, therefore, need to create an ideas of what websites they want to develop. The content shall include, text, images, videos, links to other websites, etc. A highly subsidised fee of Fifty Ghana Cedis (GH50.00) is required to be paid as prerequisite for participation. This amount will take care of all materials participants will need for the programme. This includes the final certificate to be issued after the course. Bank or Momo account details for payment will be sent to participants.

Each participant must have an email address. This will be required to sign up to Github, where participantsâ€™ finished websites will be uploaded and hosted for free. There shall be forty-five minutes lesson time, five days a week (Monday - Friday) between the hours of 12:00pm - 12:45pm and 5:30pm - 6:15pm. Participants are required to select one of the two periods provided at their convenience. The code-along approach shall be used during all the lessons. This implies, participants will be required to apply the coding elements which will be introduced during each lesson.

The office is open for any assistance between the hours of 8:00am - 6:30pm on phone, whatsapp or email (Monday - Friday.) The goal behind the online programme is to reach out to as many prospective participants as possible with interest in computer coding and be given first class tuition and guidance.

Course content materials to be used for the online programme will be made available to participants during the course. Also, codes for finished projects will be given to students to use as a guide for their future projects. The coding lessons shall be progressive and therefore missing a session may pose a challenge to a participant. In a case where a participant is unable to join a class for a valid reason, that participant may contact EasyCode for a rescheduled class.

EasyCode wishes you all a fruitful programme!
